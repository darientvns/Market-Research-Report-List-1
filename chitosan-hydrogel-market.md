<p><p>The Chitosan Hydrogel market is categorized into two main types: injection type and non-injection type. Injection type hydrogels are designed for direct delivery into tissues or organs, often utilized in medical applications such as drug delivery and wound healing, ensuring targeted treatment. In contrast, non-injection type hydrogels are typically used in topical applications, including dressings and creams, where they provide moisture retention and promote healing without invasive procedures. Both types leverage chitosan’s biocompatibility and biodegradability for effective health solutions.</p></p>
