<p><ul><li>Condensation Polymers</li><li>Addition Polymers</li><li>Hybrid Polymers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyimide binders market is categorized into three main types: condensation polymers, addition polymers, and hybrid polymers. Condensation polymers are created through the reaction of monomers that release small molecules, resulting in high thermal stability. Addition polymers, on the other hand, form through chain-growth polymerization, offering enhanced flexibility and processability. Hybrid polymers combine features of both types, providing a balance of properties like strength, thermal resistance, and chemical stability, making them versatile for various industrial applications.</p></p>
