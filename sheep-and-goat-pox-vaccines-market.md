<p><p>The Sheep and Goat Pox Vaccines market comprises two main types: live vaccines and killed vaccines. Live vaccines contain attenuated or weakened strains of the virus, inducing a strong immune response and long-lasting protection. They are often more effective and require fewer doses. Killed vaccines, on the other hand, contain inactivated virus and provide a safer alternative with a lower risk of disease transmission. However, they may necessitate multiple doses and often generate a shorter-lived immunity compared to live vaccines.</p></p>
