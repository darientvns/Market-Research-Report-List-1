<p><p>Dual-reflective window films are categorized based on their Total Solar Energy Rejected (TSER) performance, which measures the films' ability to block solar heat. The TSER ≥60% films offer a moderate level of energy rejection, suitable for residential and commercial applications where some visible light is desired. The TSER ≥70% films provide enhanced heat control, making them ideal for energy-efficient buildings. Finally, TSER ≥80% films deliver superior heat rejection, offering optimal performance for environments needing maximum climate control and comfort.</p></p>
