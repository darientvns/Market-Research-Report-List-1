<p><p>Personal Flotation Devices (PFDs) encompass various types designed to enhance safety in water activities. The buoyancy aid, typically featuring a foam core, provides basic flotation support for non-swimmers. Life jackets offer advanced buoyancy and are essential for emergency situations. Survival suits protect users from hypothermia while providing buoyancy in extreme conditions. Buoyancy compensators are specialized for divers, balancing buoyancy underwater. Other PFD types include inflatable devices and work vests, catering to specific needs in diverse marine environments.</p></p>
