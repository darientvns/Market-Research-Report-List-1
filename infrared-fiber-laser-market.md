<p><strong>The Infrared Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Type</li><li>Impulse Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared fiber laser market is categorized into continuous type and impulse type lasers. Continuous type lasers provide a constant beam for applications requiring steady energy output, ideal for cutting and welding. In contrast, impulse type lasers generate short, high-energy bursts, suitable for marking, engraving, and precision tasks. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, reflecting varying operational efficiencies and applications in sectors like manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical technology. The choice between them depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
