<p><p>Drop-in fuels for aviation include various types designed to seamlessly replace conventional jet fuels without requiring alterations to existing aircraft or infrastructure. HEFA-SPK (Hydroprocessed Esters and Fatty Acids Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) derives from renewable lipid sources. FT-SPK (Fischer-Tropsch Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) originates from gasification of biomass or fossil fuels. ATJ-SPK (Alcohol-to-Jet Synthetic Paraffinic Kerosene) converts alcohol into jet fuel. Co-processing involves blending biofeedstocks with traditional refining processes. Other methods may include innovative biofuels or emerging synthetic techniques, broadening the sustainable aviation fuel landscape.</p></p>
