<p><p>The plasma cutting torches market is categorized into high frequency and low frequency cutting torches. High frequency cutting torches utilize high voltage to create an arc, providing precise cuts and suitable for thinner materials. They are often used in industrial applications requiring speed and accuracy. In contrast, low frequency cutting torches operate at lower voltages, offering greater stability and control, making them ideal for thicker materials and applications where a smoother finish is essential. Each type serves specific cutting needs and industries.</p></p>
