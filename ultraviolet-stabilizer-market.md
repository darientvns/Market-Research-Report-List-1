<p><p>The ultraviolet stabilizer market comprises several types, primarily including UV absorbers (UVA), hindered amine light stabilizers (HALS), and others. UV absorbers absorb harmful UV radiation, protecting materials from degradation. HALS act by scavenging free radicals generated by UV exposure, extending the lifespan of products. Other stabilizers may include additives with unique properties tailored for specific applications. Together, these stabilizers enhance the durability and performance of polymers, coatings, and films in various industries, including automotive, construction, and packaging.</p></p>
