<p><p>The ant killer market features several types, including spray, powder, liquid, and gel bait. Spray formulations offer immediate contact kill and are ideal for visible infestations. Powder products target ants through contact and ingestion, providing lasting effects in hidden areas. Liquid ant killers generally attract ants for consumption, effectively eliminating colonies over time. Gel baits combine attractants with slow-acting poisons, allowing worker ants to transport the bait back to the nest, leading to colony eradication while minimizing risk to humans and pets.</p></p>
